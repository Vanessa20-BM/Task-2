DATA VISUALIZATION AND STORYTELLING – BUSINESS INSIGHTS OVERVIEW
The Superstore sales dataset was analyzed using Power BI to identify sales trends, profitability patterns, and regional performance. Various visualizations were created to support business decision-making. 

KEY FINDINGS 
Technology is the highest-performing category in terms of both sales and profit. Furniture generates the lowest profit among the major product categories. The West region contributes the highest share of overall sales. Total company sales amount to approximately 2.3 million. Total company profit amounts to approximately 286.4 thousand. 

BUSINESS RECOMMENDATION
Continue investing in Technology products due to their strong sales and profitability. Review pricing, discount strategies, and operating costs within the Furniture category to improve profitability. Analyze successful sales practices in the West region and replicate them in lower-performing regions. Monitor product-level performance to identify additional opportunities for growth and inventory optimization. 

CONCLUSION 
The analysis shows that Technology products and the West region are the primary drivers of business performance. By addressing low-profit categories and leveraging successful sales regions, the company can further improve profitability and overall growth
